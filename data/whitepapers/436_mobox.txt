About Mobox
Introduction
MOBOX is the leading web3 gaming platform featuring the co-created and co-governed open world economy. As a community-driven platform, MOBOX revolutionizes gaming through user-generated content and decentralized governance. MOBOX was listed through Binance Launchpool in 2021.
Mobox is committed to realizing the dream we've nurtured since day one, transforming the onchain gaming landscape and revolutionize how players interact within the world.
